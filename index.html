    Radar

    The MiG-29 is equipped with a useable radar and a lacking RWR system.

    It is recommended to keep it turned off most of the time when not actively in combat, to avoid detection by powerful RWRs on enemy aircraft.

    You mainly want to spot enemies visually and use the radar to identify them (if you are not sure if it's a friendly due to bad team communication).

    After locating the enemy, you can switch to IRST and lock them up. Since the IRST is a passive system, it will not give away your position.

    Launch your R27ET at no longer than 6km range in side/front aspect and 4km in rear aspect (if actively flying away from you).

    Only use the R73E at point blank range or in a dogfight.

    Enemies

    What enemies you'll face depends on the current EC bracket you are in.

    The brackets change every 2 days. So I recommed switching your plane to be at the top of the bracket.

    Most enemies you'll face are going to beat you in a dogfight, so you want to avoid that at all costs.

    Strategies

    EL RATTA - The most effective strategy is to fly low and fast, using the terrain to mask your approach sling your missles and escape.

    You can outun most enemies at the deck, so use that to your advantage.

    Never dogfight, you will lose.

    If you are in a dogfight, try to go for a one circle fight. (nose to nose)

    You can get your nose on target quicker than most enemies. so you can shoot off an R73E and run.

    Summary

    You can be very effective in the Mig-29, but you have to play smarter than your western counterparts.

    Also NEVER play the 13.0 to 13.7 bracket, you will get rawdogged
